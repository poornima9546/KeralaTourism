About Kottayam

Kottayam is a city in the Indian state of Kerala, flanked by the Western Ghats on the east and the Vembanad Lake and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west. It is the district headquarters of Kottayam district, located in south-west Kerala. Kottayam is located in the basin of the Meenachil River at an average elevation of 3 metres (9.8 ft) above sea level, and has a moderate climate. It is located approximately 155 kilometres (96 mi) north of the state capital Thiruvananthapuram. Kottayam has an average elevation of 3 metres (9.8 ft) above sea level.[11] It is situated in the basin of the Meenachil River and in the basin of the Vembanad backwaters, which are formed from several streams in the Western Ghats of the Idukki district. The city is situated near the inland estuary of Meenachil river where it empties into the Vembanad lake in Kumarakom.


Top Tourist Attractions

Marmala Waterfall

Marmala Waterfall is situated in the Marmala village, which is part of the Wagamon hill station in the Idukki district of Kerala. Marmala Waterfall is known for its impressive height, and the water cascades down through rocky terrain.

Kottathavalam

Kottathavalam is a place of archaeological interest at the legends says that the Royal Family of Madurai would ascend a rock cut path to take rest in this ancient cave.

Vembanad Lake

Vembanad is the longest lake in India and the largest lake in the state of Kerala. It is one of the famous backwater lakes in Kerala and is known for its scenic beauty and diverse ecosystem. Vembanad Lake spans several districts, including Alappuzha, Kottayam, and Ernakulam.

Aruvikkuzhi Waterfalls

The waterfall is known for its natural beauty and serene surroundings. It is a popular spot for nature lovers and tourists seeking a peaceful retreat.The water flows down a rocky terrain, creating small pools at different levels.The best time to visit is during the monsoon season when the waterfall is in its full glory.

Ettumanoor Mahadeva Temple

Ettumanoor Mahadeva Temple is a prominent Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in the town of Ettumanoor in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India. The temple is adorned with mural paintings that depict various episodes from Hindu mythology.

Kumarakom Backwaters

Kumarakom is a beautiful village in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India, known for its enchanting backwaters and serene landscapes. The backwaters of Kumarakom are a popular tourist destination, offering a tranquil and picturesque escape.
